Stockholms Stadsmission is a Swedish non-profit organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable individuals and communities in Stockholm. Founded in 1853, its mission is to combat homelessness, poverty, social exclusion, and isolation through a wide range of services and initiatives. The organization provides essential support such as shelters, food distribution, counseling, healthcare, and educational programs.

Stockholms Stadsmission is committed to creating a more inclusive society, where everyone has access to basic needs, opportunities, and dignity. By working with individuals, families, and the local community, the organization seeks to empower people to rebuild their lives and reintegrate into society.
